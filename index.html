Hello, thanks for stopping by!
I have always been deeply passionate about mathematics as I find there is an inherent beauty in the subject – whether it be in expressing abstract problems or the geometric properties of complex numbers, e.g., the Mandelbrot set. Consequently, this passion has led me to study the subject at a higher level at McMaster University.
Likewise, I have a keen interest in computer science. In High School, I was fortunate enough to be able to take a programming class where I learned Java. For a year-end project I wrote a program, with a complete GUI, that allowed a user to play blackjack against the computer. Following High School, I stopped studying computer science and it was only after studying mathematics, was my interest later rekindled.
With continued advancements in machine learning and AI, mathematics and statistics will play an ever-larger role in our future. A future that I intended to be part of it.
