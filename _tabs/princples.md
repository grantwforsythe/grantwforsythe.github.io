My Life Principles:
1. There is meaning in suffering.
2. Take responsibility for your actions and inactions. 
3. Be honest with yourself and with others.
4. Be precise in your speech. 
5. Compare yourself with who you were yesterday, not with who someone else is today.
6. Surround yourself with people who want the best for you.
7. Listen to others as if they have something meaningful to say. 
8. "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful." - Warren Buffet

**Note**: Several of these rules are from Dr. Jordan Peterson's books: [12 Rules for Life](https://www.amazon.ca/12-Rules-Life-Antidote-Chaos/dp/0345816021/ref=pd_bxgy_img_2/143-7611967-8937513?_encoding=UTF8&pd_rd_i=0345816021&pd_rd_r=7c874caa-d3f1-463e-b2e2-67f4dfd3a067&pd_rd_w=RrzuM&pd_rd_wg=UN1o9&pf_rd_p=cef4e95a-d595-4ed7-b25a-14b0a9e4059c&pf_rd_r=VF7YQGNJQGEFQBSARSYT&psc=1&refRID=VF7YQGNJQGEFQBSARSYT) and [Beyond Order](https://www.amazon.ca/Beyond-Order-More-Rules-Life/dp/0735278334/ref=sr_1_1?dchild=1&keywords=jordan+peterson&qid=1618605700&sr=8-1).
